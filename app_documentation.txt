EXPENSEEYE - APP OVERVIEW & FEATURES

ExpenseEye is a personal finance tracker designed to help you monitor your spending, manage budgets, track debts, and maintain shopping checklists.

--- MAIN PAGES (TABS) ---

1. DASHBOARD (The Home Page)
- Opens automatically when you start the app.
- FEATURES:
  - Big Spending Summary: See exactly how much you've spent this month at a glance.
  - Quick Comparison: Tells you if you are spending more or less than last month.
  - Overview Grid: Shows today's, this week's, and this year's total spending.
  - Budget Progress: Visual bars showing how much of your monthly budget you've used up.
  - Recent Expenses: A quick list of your 5 most recent transactions.
  - Floating "+" Button: Tap to quickly add a new expense from anywhere on this page.
  - Hamburger Menu (Top Right): Access advanced tools like Monthly Budgets, Reminder Expenses, and Borrow/Owe.

2. EXPENSES (Detailed History)
- Opens when you tap the second icon in the bottom menu.
- FEATURES:
  - Full List: Shows every single expense you've ever recorded, grouped by date.
  - Search Bar: Type to find specific expenses by their title or description.
  - Filters: Tap the filter icon to find expenses by a specific date range, category, payment method, or amount.
  - Tap to Edit: Tap any item to change its details or delete it.

3. CHECKLIST (Shopping List)
- Opens when you tap the third icon in the bottom menu.
- FEATURES:
  - Add Items: Quickly type items you need to buy (like "Milk" or "Bread").
  - Smart Category: The app tries to guess the category (Dairy, Grocery, etc.) as you type.
  - Shopping Mode: A clean mode that makes it easier to check off items while in a store.
  - Log as Expense: When you check an item as "done," the app asks if you want to automatically add it to your daily expenses so you don't have to type it again.

4. REPORTS (Visual Analysis)
- Opens when you tap the fourth icon in the bottom menu.
- FEATURES:
  - Statistics: See your daily average spending and your "Top Category" (where your most money goes).
  - Pie Chart: A colorful circle showing which categories take up the biggest slice of your budget.
  - Spending Trend: A line graph showing how your spending goes up or down over time.
  - Payment Bar Chart: Shows which payment methods (Cash, UPI, Card) you use most.
  - Range Selector: Switch between This Month, Last Month, This Year, or All Time views.

5. SETTINGS (Personalization)
- Opens when you tap the last icon in the bottom menu.
- FEATURES:
  - Appearance: Choose between 4 beautiful themes (Midnight, Forest, Sand, Ocean) and toggle Dark Mode.
  - Currency: Change your currency symbol (₹, $, €, etc.).
  - Daily Log Reminder: Set a specific time to receive a daily notification to remind you to log your expenses.
  - Category Management: Create your own categories with custom icons and colors.
  - Smart Features: Toggle title suggestions and auto-classification on or off.
  - Tools & Data:
    - Export: Save your data as a PDF report or a CSV spreadsheet.
    - Backup: Save your database to your phone or cloud storage.
    - Restore: Load an old backup. You can choose to "Merge" (add to current data) or "Overwrite" (replace current data).
  - Test Notification: A button to check if the app's notification system is working correctly on your phone.

--- SPECIAL FEATURES ---

6. MONTHLY BUDGETS (Spending Limits)
- Accessible via the Dashboard hamburger menu.
- FEATURE: Set a limit for how much you want to spend in a month (Overall or per Category). The Dashboard will show you how close you are to hitting that limit.

7. REMINDER EXPENSES (Auto-Logging)
- Accessible via the Dashboard hamburger menu.
- FEATURE: Schedule repeating bills (like Rent, Netflix, or EMI). The app will automatically log these as expenses on the due date and notify you.

8. BORROW & OWE (Debt Tracker)
- Accessible via the Dashboard hamburger menu.
- FEATURE: Track money you've borrowed from someone or lent to a friend.
  - Contact Integration: Quickly pick names from your phone's contacts.
  - Due Dates: Set a date for when the money should be returned.
  - Reminders: Get a notification when a debt is due.
  - Partial Payments: Record when someone pays you back a small amount over time.
  - Resolve: Mark a debt as "Settled" when it's fully paid back.

--- HOW THE APP WORKS (LOGIC) ---

- OFFLINE FIRST: All your data stays on your phone. No internet required.
- AUTOMATIC SAVING: Everything saves the moment you tap "Save" or "Done."
- SMART GUESSING: The app learns from your previous entries to suggest titles and categories.
- RESTART PROTECTION: If you restart your phone, the app automatically reschedules your reminders so you never miss a bill.
- DATA SAFETY: Using the "Merge" restore option ensures you never lose new data when importing an old backup.
